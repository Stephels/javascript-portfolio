About.

My Career

I am an enthusiastic full-stack web developer in training, passionate about merging technology, design, and creativity to craft user-focused and innovative software solutions. My journey in technology has been fueled by an intensive Full-Stack Web Development program, where I’ve gained hands-on experience with core technologies like JavaScript, HTML, CSS, React, MongoDB, Git, and Node.js. In addition, I’ve explored specialized frameworks, such as Next.js, to deepen my expertise and strengthen my capabilities as a developer.

My diverse background in art, graphic design, and Communications, paired with Web Design certifications, provides a creative edge in my approach to development projects. This combination allows me to ensure my work isn’t just functional, but also visually engaging and user-centric.

Things I'm doing

Currently, I am diving deep into projects that push the boundaries of web development, actively sharpening my skills in modern tools and technologies. I’m focusing on building real-world applications, collaborating with teams, and experimenting with frameworks and methodologies that broaden my practical experience. Alongside my technical training, I leverage my experience in social media, marketing, and sales to integrate SEO strategies and effective content creation into my work.

Fun Fact

I’ve been immersed in the wine industry for over a decade, and I’m currently expanding my knowledge through short courses at The Cape Wine Academy. The history, science, and art of wine production continue to fascinate me.

The Journey Ahead

Looking ahead, I am excited to contribute to impactful projects and explore opportunities in mobile development, leadership roles, and new frontiers in the tech industry. My goal is to keep growing both as a developer and as a collaborator, always striving to create solutions that leave a lasting mark.

Back-End Development

Database Management
